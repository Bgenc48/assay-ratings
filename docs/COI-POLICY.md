# Conflict-of-Interest Policy

The operator of Assay is the founder of **ATA Coin**. This policy exists so
that fact never becomes a question about the integrity of any grade.

1. **ATA Coin is Not Rated.** Its page (when it deploys) shows the raw
   dimension facts and claim verdicts computed by the same public pipeline
   as every other token — and no letter grade, permanently. The registry
   entry is pinned `coi: true`; the scanner enforces the carve-out in code
   (`apps/scanner/src/run.js`).
2. **Competitor tokens are graded by algorithmic output only.** Human
   overrides on the grade of any token that competes directly with ATA are
   prohibited. Their report pages carry an automatic disclosure.
3. **No trading around rating actions.** The operator does not trade any
   rated token within 7 days either side of that token's rating change, and
   never holds short positions in rated tokens.
4. **Site-wide disclosure.** The operator's token holdings and affiliations
   are disclosed on the public policies page and updated on change.
   Current: founder allocation of ATA Coin (pre-deployment), subject to a
   published 12-month cliff / 5-year vesting schedule in the ATA-Coin
   repository.
5. **Origin disclosed.** Assay's methodology grew out of the adversarial
   trust-gap review originally built for ATA Coin. That is a disclosed fact,
   cited in the founding research (docs/research/).
6. **No Assay token, ever. No issuer-pays, ever.** (See policies page —
   these are independence rules, restated here because they interact with
   every conflict.)

Violations of this policy are legitimate grounds for public challenge via
the dispute process, and confirmed violations will be recorded permanently
in docs/CORRECTIONS.md.
